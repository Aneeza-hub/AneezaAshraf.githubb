# Final Reflection

Throughout this course, I experienced significant growth both academically and professionally. The Capstone & Professional Practices course helped me understand the importance of combining technical skills with soft skills such as communication, teamwork, and critical thinking.

One of the most important things I learned is that software development is not just about writing code, but about solving real-world problems that impact society. My project on the “Impacts of Social Media on Mental Health” helped me understand how technology can influence human behavior in both positive and negative ways.

This course also improved my ability to work in a structured environment, manage tasks, and reflect on my learning journey. I became more aware of ethical responsibilities in computing, especially regarding user privacy and digital well-being.

The most valuable lesson I learned is the importance of continuous learning. In the field of Computer Science, technologies evolve rapidly, so it is necessary to keep updating skills and knowledge.

In the future, I aim to apply everything I have learned in real-world projects and further develop my skills in software development, Artificial Intelligence, and Cybersecurity.

Overall, this course has been a very meaningful experience that helped me grow as a student and prepared me for my professional career.
