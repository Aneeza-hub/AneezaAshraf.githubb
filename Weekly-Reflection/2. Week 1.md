# Week 1 — Reflection

---

**Topic Covered:** Computer Science in Society

---

## What I Learned

This week introduced the broader role of Computer Science in shaping modern society, spanning healthcare, education, communication, and business. A key takeaway was that technology extends far beyond writing code; it is fundamentally about solving real-world problems and improving people's quality of life. I also gained an understanding of the responsibility developers carry in designing systems that are ethical, inclusive, and considerate of the end user.

---

## Key Insight

Every piece of technology we create carries a social dimension. As developers, it is essential to look beyond functionality and consider the broader impact on users, communities, and ethical standards throughout the software development process.

---

## Real-Life Application

This understanding directly informs how I approach software projects. Designing applications that prioritize data privacy, accessibility, and ethical considerations is not optional,  it is a professional obligation. Going forward, I intend to incorporate these principles into every stage of development, from planning to deployment.
