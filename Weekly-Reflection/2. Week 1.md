# **Week 1**

## **Topic Covered**

Computer Science in Society

## **What I Learned**

This week, I learned how Computer Science impacts different areas of society such as healthcare, education, communication, and business. It helped me understand that technology is not just about coding, but about solving real-world problems and improving the quality of life for people. I also learned that developers have a responsibility to design ethical and user-friendly systems.

## **Key Insight**

The most important insight was that every technology we create has a social impact, so it is necessary to think about users, ethics, and society while developing software.

## **Real-life Application**

This learning can be applied in real projects by designing applications that are secure, user-friendly, and ethical. For example, ensuring data privacy and accessibility while developing software systems.
