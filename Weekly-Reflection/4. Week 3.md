# Week 3 — Reflection

---

**Topic Covered:** Personal Branding

---

## What I Learned

This week highlighted the critical role of personal branding in establishing a credible and recognizable professional identity. Platforms such as LinkedIn and GitHub serve as powerful tools for showcasing skills, projects, and achievements to prospective employers and collaborators. I also gained a clearer understanding of how personal branding shapes the way others perceive and evaluate a professional profile in competitive environments.

---

## Key Insight

A well-crafted personal brand is more than a digital presence — it is a strategic asset. Professionals with a strong and consistent brand are better positioned to stand out in the industry and attract meaningful career opportunities.

---

## Real-Life Application

I can actively apply this by keeping my LinkedIn profile current and professionally presented, publishing projects on GitHub to demonstrate practical ability, and being intentional about building a coherent and credible online presence over time.
