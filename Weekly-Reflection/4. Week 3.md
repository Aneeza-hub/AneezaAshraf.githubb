# **Week 3**

## **Topic Covered**

Personal Branding

## **What I Learned**

This week, I learned the importance of personal branding in building a professional identity. Platforms like LinkedIn and GitHub help showcase skills, projects, and achievements to potential employers. I also learned that personal branding reflects how others perceive your professional profile.

## **Key Insight**

The key insight was that a strong personal brand can open better career opportunities and make a candidate stand out in the industry.

## **Real-life Application**

I can apply this by maintaining an updated LinkedIn profile, uploading projects on GitHub, and building a professional online presence.
