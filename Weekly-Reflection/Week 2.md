# **Week 2**

## **Topic Covered**

Workplace Culture

## **What I Learned**

This week helped me understand the importance of workplace culture in professional environments. I learned that a positive workplace encourages teamwork, communication, respect, and productivity. I also understood how important it is to behave professionally and adapt to different working environments.

## **Key Insight**

A key insight was that success in the workplace depends not only on technical skills but also on communication, behavior, and teamwork.

## **Real-life Application**

In real-world jobs, I can apply this by maintaining professionalism, respecting colleagues, and contributing positively in team-based projects.
