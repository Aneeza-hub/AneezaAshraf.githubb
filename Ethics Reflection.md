# ⚖️ Ethics Reflection

## **Ethical Issue Chosen**

An important ethical issue related to my project is the **impact of social media on mental health**. Many social media platforms are designed to increase user engagement, which can sometimes lead to excessive usage and addiction, negatively affecting users’ mental well-being.

---

## **Problem Explanation**

The main issue is that social media platforms often use algorithms that encourage users to spend more time online. This can lead to problems such as anxiety, stress, low self-esteem, and reduced productivity, especially among young users. Users may also compare themselves with others, which further impacts their mental health.

---

## **Relevant Professional Code**

This issue is related to the **ACM Code of Ethics**, which highlights the responsibility of computing professionals to:

* Avoid harm to users
* Ensure systems are designed with user well-being in mind
* Promote transparency and responsible use of technology

---

## **My Decision & Justification**

As a future developer, I believe it is important to design systems that prioritize user well-being instead of only focusing on engagement. Developers should consider features like usage limits, content warnings, and transparent algorithms to reduce negative psychological effects.

---

## **Conclusion**

Ethical responsibility is essential in software development, especially in applications that influence human behavior. Technology should support mental well-being, not harm it.
