# 💻 Project Reflection

## **Project Title**

Impacts of Social Media on Mental Health

---

## **My Role in the Project**

In this project, I contributed as a developer and researcher. My role involved understanding the problem domain, analyzing how social media affects mental health, and supporting the development of solutions based on findings. I also helped in organizing information and ensuring the project was aligned with the objectives.

---

## **Project Overview**

The main purpose of this project is to study how social media usage impacts mental health, especially among young users. It focuses on both positive and negative effects such as communication benefits, addiction, anxiety, stress, and self-esteem issues.

---

## **Challenges Faced**

During the project, we faced several challenges such as:

* Understanding the psychological impact of social media
* Collecting reliable and relevant data
* Managing time effectively while balancing other coursework
* Coordinating tasks within the team

---

## **How Challenges Were Solved**

We overcame these challenges through research, discussion, and teamwork. We referred to academic articles and real-world studies to better understand the topic. Proper planning and division of tasks helped us manage time efficiently.

---

## **What I Learned**

This project helped me understand the real-world impact of technology on human behavior. I learned how social media can influence mental health and how important it is to design responsible digital systems. I also improved my teamwork, communication, and problem-solving skills.

---

## **Conclusion**

Overall, this project was a valuable learning experience that enhanced my understanding of both technical and social aspects of computing. It helped me connect theoretical knowledge with real-world applications.
